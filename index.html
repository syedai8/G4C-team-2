Nectar Saga

Published By Hussain A, Syeda I, Katrina K

GAMEPLAY

Starting the game, you are given a bee, a hive, and a flower. Using the bee to source nectar, the bee pollinates the land, improving vegetation. Evolution occurs when nectar quotas are met, making the bee more efficient.

PITCH

Nectar Saga is a strategy game where players take on the character Inaya, who is a bee caretaker guiding her bees to collect nectar, pollinate the land, and support local communities. Players make important decisions, such as which bees to evolve, when to harvest, and how to manage resources—to meet nectar quotas for her neighborhood to thrive. Adding fun gameplay with a social impact, Nectar Saga teaches players about collaboration, sustainability, and the power of small actions in creating a big change.

THEME

Our focus is outgrowing hunger while promoting community, collaboration, and decision-making. Users make choices for Inaya and her neighborhood to meet in-game goals.

OUR WHY

Inaya uses her bees and garden to support families, persevering through long processes to benefit her community.
